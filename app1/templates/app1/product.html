Coconut Oil

Famous coconut oil is 100% pure and edible natural oil. Contains trans fatty acids not more than 0% by weight, and saturated fatty acids not more than 91% b weight. Coconut oil solidifies below 25 0 C/77 F. At around 30 0 C/86 F, it melts and becomes liquid again. Due to various climatic conditions prevailing over a period of time, wax like granules could be seen in it. It is a natural occurrence. The granules becomes liquid again when heated above 40 0 C/104 0 F
